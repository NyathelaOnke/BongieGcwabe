About Me




I am a Registered Private Social Worker, an Executive & Life Coach and a Facilitator

I have been a Social Worker and a Wellness Specialist for more than thirty years, and have assisted my clients to deal appropriately with different challenges that impact negatively in their lives, in order to improve their well-being.

Coaching

I have also discovered that there are people that present themselves for counselling, believing they have psycho-social or emotional challenges, struggling to align their goals personally, and professionally with those of their organisations. Something that results in uncertainty and despair. These people tend to benefit more from coaching, a service I also provide.

My coaching process involves identifying your strengths and opportunities, to navigate through your goals. A client-centred coaching framework is applied throughout this process. If I have never been on the same path with you, I use questioning techniques that emanate from a curious mind that is free from biases, to assist you to achieve your goals. Through coaching, hope is certain.

My consultations are face-to-face and virtual (Telephone and Zoom/Skype/Microsoft Teams). I am based in Pretoria, South Africa.

Confidentiality is the cornerstone of all my services.






Coaching at work

My Services

Organisations

Employee Health and Wellness (EHW) Services

Employees are assisted to cope emotionally and psychologically with their challenges, which impact negatively on their work performance and/or social functioning. Such interventions assist to enhance organisations’ efficiency. The EHW services are also provided as an extension to the employees’ families.

Emotional Impact sessions (EIs)

EIs provide a platform for employees to deal with their challenges personally and in their work environment. The sessions further give employees an opportunity to express their feelings without fear of being judged or intimidated. The focus is on containing the participants so that they can ‘normalise’ their painful experiences and explore coping strategies in order to manage their stress and become more resilient.

Executive and Management Coaching

Through Executive and Management Coaching, the Coachees are assisted to align their goals with the organisational culture and climate for personal effectiveness and the company’s efficiency.

COVID-19 Support Services

  • The Infected (Online Emotional and Psychological Support during and beyond the disease)

  • The Affected (Online Stress and Anxiety Management Techniques, Dealing with loss through
    death, etc)

  • Remote working due to Comorbidities (Online Lifestyle and Mental Health Support)

Team Wellness Audits
The focus is on assisting the team to identify its short comings, coping strategies, and strengths. An assessment report with recommendations on strengthening the team for peak performance is compiled for the employer.

Any other service that is informed by the organisation’s needs

Individual Services

Therapeutic Counselling services for individuals experiencing life challenges that impact negatively on their functioning and/or total wellbeing. Examples include, but are not limited to: stress related issues, relationships, trauma, loss of a loved one, divorce, abuse, harassment, etc.

There are instances when you would think you need counselling for your challenges only to find that after an assessment, you could benefit more through Life Coaching. If so, you will be at the right place to continue being assisted by me through collaboration, so that you can explore your options and navigate through your goals on the personal and professional levels.

Groups Intervention

Therapeutic Counselling Services are also provided to a group of people, depending on their preferences and feasibility. For example; colleagues that are survivors of cancer could be assisted to form a support group, colleagues that have lost one of their own tragically, could benefit from a Group Trauma Debriefing session. The group members that need individual follow-ups are also identified during the sessions and assisted afterwards.
